What it does

Built for people who collect documents from many web pages — researchers, public-records archivists, internal-portal users, designers, students, and anyone tired of clicking "Save as…" hundreds of times.

🔎Finds files others miss

Detects iframe-embedded PDFs, lazy-loaded images, fetch/XHR file responses, blob URLs, CMS download routes, hidden form values, and 30+ data-* attributes that plain link scrapers ignore.

⚙️Smart filtering

Filter results by extension, file size, file name, page title, or modification date. Sort, manually reorder, and copy URLs as plain text.

🕸️Same-site crawl

Choose a depth (0–5 or unlimited) and visit every same-domain link to surface PDFs on subpages, viewer URLs, and CMS document routes — up to 100,000 pages per run.

📚Local PDF merge

Combine selected PDFs into a single file with one click. No upload, no server. Your files stay on your device.

🗜️ZIP archive

Bundle selected files into a single ZIP for tidy distribution, archiving, or sharing — generated entirely in your browser.

📊CSV export

Export URLs, file names, sizes, types, and titles to CSV for documentation, audit, inventory, or downstream automation.

現在のタブを使うか、ポップアップにURLを貼り付け。クロール深度を「このページのみ」「同一サイト内1〜5段」「無制限」から選びます。

検出可能なファイルが一覧表示される

ファイル名、種類、サイズ、ページタイトル、ソースURLを確認できます。保存前に絞り込み・並べ替え・検索・手動並べ替えが可能。

選んで保存・ZIP化・PDF結合

指定フォルダへ一括保存（種類別・ドメイン別整理オプションあり）、ZIPにまとめる、選択したPDFを1つに結合 — すべてブラウザ内で完結。
